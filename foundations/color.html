Button hierarchy. Scarcity rule.

Five tiers · Signal is rare · Terracotta is the default primary

The logo uses Signal. The single hero CTA uses Signal. Everything else uses terracotta, brick red, ghost, or neutral. Most product screens have zero Signal buttons. If a screen has two Signal buttons, one is wrong.

1 · Hero
Signal. Once per screen, max. Landing-page CTA only.
2 · Primary
Terracotta. Default positive action in product surfaces.
3 · Destructive
Brick red. Irreversible or system-state-changing actions.
4 · Ghost
Secondary or dismiss. Border + transparent fill.
5 · Neutral
Warm-700. Low-emphasis persist or submit.
